About

I'm David, a videographer and IT student based in Perth. I started out as a content creator and FPV drone pilot, then picked up a camera and never put it down.

Today I run Davisual Studio, producing video for local cafes, restaurants and events. Alongside that I'm completing my Certificate IV in IT (Programming) at North Metro TAFE, learning to build the kind of sites you're looking at right now.

My goal is to grow Davisual Studio from a solo operation into a proper creative agency.
